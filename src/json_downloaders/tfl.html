Railcard

If you have a:

  • Senior Railcard
  • 16-25 Railcard
  • Disabled Persons Railcard
  • Gold Card
  • HM Forces Railcard

You can get:

  • 34 per cent off off-peak Oyster single fares on all rail services in London
  • 34 per cent off off-peak daily price cap
  • Discounted Off-Peak Day Travelcards (also for Network Railcard and Family and Friends Railcard holders)

To get the discounted Oyster fares you need to register your Railcard details on your Oyster card. You can do this at Tube, London Overground and some National Rail ticket offices.
